About

I am a third year Textile and Apparel Design student at the National Institute of Design, Andhra Pradesh. My practice sits at the intersection of material research, craft, and design — with a particular interest in understanding what fibres can become beyond their conventional applications. I am drawn to processes that are slow and considered — weaving, embroidery, surface design — and to the questions that emerge when you work closely with material.
