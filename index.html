About

Alma Rachel Ipe

I am a third year Textile and Apparel Design student at the National Institute of Design, Andhra Pradesh. What draws me most to textiles is the patience and process behind it all.


Weaving holds a special place in my practice. There is something deeply satisfying about watching a textile take shape one pick at a time. But I am equally curious about every other part of the textile design process, from surface development to pattern drafting to apparel construction.


Beyond the studio I am someone who loves being on the move, whether it is hiking through new landscapes or seeking out craft communities and learning how things are made by hand. I bring that same curiosity and energy into my design practice.


I want to be a designer who creates with intention, where every material choice, every structure and every detail has a reason behind it.
